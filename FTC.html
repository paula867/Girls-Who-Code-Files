What is FTC?

FTC stand for First Tech Challnge. The goal of the challenge changes every year. Basic things like the size of the bot and the object remains the same. You are only allowed to have up to 15 people per team. If your club has more members then you will have to make another team. FTC is for grades 7-12. The cost of registering to FTC is about $2,250 with parts accountin for the robot, team and event registration and any other additional costs. Teams register in May and go to World Championchips in April. FIRST also offers scholarships to students participating in the competiton.

What positions are avaliable in FTC teams?

Coding: Your teams needs a coder(s) to program your bot. You will need to program the controls and if you can you can program sensor. Designing: Minutes of praning save hours of fixing hardware. Your team needs to think about what will be built on the bot, how, and why. Hardware: You build the bot. Leader: You oversee what other students are doing and make sure they're doing it right.
